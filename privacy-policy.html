Privacy Policy for House and Flat Rent Website

Effective Date: 18th Sep, 2023

1. Introduction

Welcome to Rentrr.in, a website dedicated to helping users find and list houses and flats for rent. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

2. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information: When you use our website or services, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and location.

b. Property Information: If you are listing a property for rent, we may collect property-related information, including but not limited to property type, location, rent amount, and property description.

c. Usage Information: We may collect information about your use of our website, including your browsing history, IP address, device information, and cookies.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

a. Providing Services: To facilitate the process of finding and listing properties for rent.

b. Communication: To communicate with you regarding your account, listings, inquiries, and updates.

c. Improvement: To analyze user behavior and preferences to improve our website and services.

d. Marketing: To send you promotional materials and updates about our services, with your consent.

4. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

a. Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website and providing our services.

b. Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information when required by law or to protect our rights, property, and safety or that of others.

c. With Your Consent: We will share your information with your explicit consent.

5. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is entirely secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

a. Access: You can request access to the personal information we hold about you.

b. Correction: You can request that we correct any inaccuracies in your personal information.

c. Deletion: You can request that we delete your personal information, subject to legal requirements.

7. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can configure your browser to refuse cookies or notify you when cookies are being sent.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices, and we encourage you to review their privacy policies.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page, and the revised policy will have an updated effective date.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at vennilak717@gmail.com.
